# 01 · Design Brief

## Product

**Diamond Riches** is a luxury-lifestyle themed HTML5 video slot. It is the
flagship ("featured") title in the *SevenSlot Web* casino-style game
collection, alongside *Triple Sevens* and *Double-Up Keno*. The three games
share one cabinet chassis and design system; Diamond Riches overrides it with
a distinct navy/cyan/gold "diamond" art direction.

## Audience & context

- **Players:** adult casual social-casino / sweepstakes players on **mobile
  web first** (portrait phone), also desktop browser.
- **Session shape:** short, high-frequency sessions; one-thumb reachable
  controls; frequent auto-play use (the player may walk away mid-run).
- **Emotional target:** aspirational opulence — private jets, yachts,
  diamonds. Premium but legible; celebratory on wins without being noisy.

## Brand pillars

1. **Opulent, not gaudy.** Deep navy field, polished cyan-diamond accents,
   restrained gold. Jewel-box, not Vegas-marquee.
2. **Always legible.** Money, bet, and state read instantly at arm's length
   in sunlight. LED-style numerics; high-contrast amber/gold on near-black.
3. **Tactile & responsive.** Every input has audio + haptic + visual
   feedback within 100 ms. Reels feel physical.
4. **Calm by default, loud on payoff.** The idle screen is quiet; BIG / MEGA
   / EPIC and the bonus are where spectacle is spent.
5. **Trustworthy.** Published RTP, visible RNG statement, responsible-gaming
   surfaces, no dark patterns in the bet/auto controls.

## The signature mechanics (what makes it *this* game)

| Mechanic | Player-facing behaviour |
|---|---|
| **Diamond WILD** | Appears on the middle three reel-bands. Substitutes for **every** symbol — including the SCATTER (a WILD counts toward scatter pays and the bonus trigger) — and **doubles** any line it completes. Carries an ornate red/gold "Double" ribbon — the cabinet's tell. |
| **Gold-Coin SCATTER** | Pays × **total** bet from anywhere; 3+ awards the bonus. |
| **Collectible-multiplier free spins** | 12 free spins. Every diamond WILD landed during the bonus is "collected" and permanently raises a persistent multiplier: starts **2×**, +1 per diamond, caps **29×** (27 diamonds). |
| **Retrigger** | 3+ scatters during the bonus add **+12** spins; the multiplier persists across retriggers. |
| **FEVER progressives** | Three globally-shared jackpots (FEVER 1/2/3) seeded $1,000 / $500 / $250, bet-scaled trigger odds, celebrated full-screen. |
| **Win tiers** | WIN ≥3×, BIG ≥10×, MEGA ≥25×, EPIC ≥50× total bet — escalating celebration + a player-acknowledged pause during auto-play on BIG+. |

## Layout intent (recently revised — important for the agency)

The cabinet was **rotated 90°** from a conventional 5×3. It now presents as
**3 columns wide × 5 rows tall**: five horizontal reel-bands that spin
**left→right**, with paylines reading **top→bottom**. Row indices 1–5 are
labelled down the left rail. Any redesign must preserve this orientation —
it is a deliberate product differentiator, and the math/paylines are encoded
around it.

## Tone of voice (UI copy)

Terse, confident, premium. "SPIN", "MAX", "FREE SPINS", "TAP TO START".
Celebratory copy is short and capitalized ("★ EPIC WIN ★",
"12 Free Spins Won!"). Responsible-gaming copy is plain and calm.

## Success criteria for a redesign

- Preserves the 3×5 / top→bottom reading and all 20 payline shapes.
- Keeps every state legible at 360 px wide in bright light.
- Maintains the BIG+ auto-play pause and the bonus tap-to-proceed gates
  (these are UX safety affordances, not decoration).
- Reduced-motion and keyboard paths remain first-class.
- All money/bet math stays exactly representable (1 credit = $0.01).
