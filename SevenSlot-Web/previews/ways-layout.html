5×3 WAYS GAME — LAYOUT PREVIEW

3 cols · 5 rows · 15 mini-reels · row-major stop · 125 ways
Press SPIN to see the layout in action
Each cell is its own independent mini-reel. All 15 begin spinning at once; they stop one at a time in numbered order. Once 2+ JP symbols are visible on stopped cells, remaining cells enter SWEAT mode — slower stop, gold pulse, intensity escalates per landed JP. Trigger thresholds: 3 JP = JP3, 4 = JP2, 5 = JP1. Only one tier fires.

JP HOLD RULE: after 2 JP are already on board, every cell that lands an additional JP holds in the spinning state for at least 10 seconds before revealing. The trigger moment never feels cheap.
